Sound waves that dissolve dirt in minutes.

Ultrasonic cleaning uses high-frequency sound waves to create millions of microscopic bubbles that implode on contact with dirt, oils, and buildup — reaching every crevice a brush never could. It's the same technology used by professional jewelers worldwide.
